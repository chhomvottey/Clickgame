Our Vistion

Welcome to RabBitcoin we envision a future where gaming and cryptocurrency converge to create a vibrant and rewarding community. We aim to provide players with real economic opportunities through engaging Play-to-Earn (P2E) games, where every action can lead to tangible rewards. Our mission is to empower users with a seamless and enjoyable gaming experience while maximizing the value of their time and efforts.

Roadmap



Core Game Development

  • Mechanics & Graphics: Finalize essential game elements, from gameplay mechanics to visuals, making sure they are appealing and user-friendly.

Blockchain Integration

  • Token Economy: Implement an in-game currency or token system, allowing players to earn, spend, and trade within the game.
  • NFT Ownership: Enable NFT functionality for unique in-game assets, providing players with real ownership of items they acquire.
  • Smart Contract Security: Integrate secure and reliable smart contracts to handle transactions, prioritizing the protection of user assets.

Seed Round & Investor Engagement

  • Funding Launch: Initiate a seed funding round to attract initial investors, securing capital to drive further development.
  • Strategic Partnerships: Target investors with blockchain and gaming expertise who can support both financial and growth needs, enhancing the game's visibility in the market.
